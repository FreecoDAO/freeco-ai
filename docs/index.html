What is FreEco.ai

Not a chatbot. An operating system for agents.

Traditional agent frameworks wait for you to type. FreEco.ai runs autonomous agents that work for you: on schedules, around the clock — building knowledge, monitoring targets, generating leads, managing daily life, and reporting to your dashboard. Built on the battle-tested OpenFang Agent OS with 16 security levels and ethical, ecological guidelines.

1single binary — one install, agents live
137Klines of Rust across 14 crates
2,696+tests passing, zero clippy warnings
16security levels, ethics built in
Four Editions

One binary. Configured for your life.

🧑‍💼

Personal Concierge

Hi-end Swiss-style assistant for life, work, travel, shopping and health coaching. Runs 24/7 and manages your day autonomously.

🧒

Kids Edition

Ethical, ecological, child-safe AI companion for study and online security. Strictly guardrailed, COPPA-aligned, with parental visibility.

🏢

Business Suite

Autonomous buyer and executive-agent team for shops, restaurants and non-profits: leads, competitor intel, scheduling, reporting.

🤖

AI Company

A self-running AI company on your device. Agent teams that plan, delegate, execute and report — end to end.
